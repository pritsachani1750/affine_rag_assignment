1. Purpose: Sterilization process.
2. Scope: Reusable components.
3. Responsibilities: Operators perform cleaning.
4. Cleaning: Use enzymatic detergent.
5. Rinsing: Use deionized water.
6. Sterilization: Autoclave at 134°C for 18 minutes.
7. Verification: Record batch ID and operator.
8. Failed Cycles: Repeat sterilization.
9. Storage: Store in sealed pouches.
10. Shelf Life: Use within 30 days.
11. Disposal: Discard damaged pouches.
12. Records: Maintain sterilization logs.
13. Audit: Review logs monthly.
14. Deviations: Escalate to Quality.
15. References: ISO procedures.
